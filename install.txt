Lumi StockHub
Version 1.0.0

Requirements
-------------
- Quasar Smartphone V3
- ESX Legacy
- ox_lib
- oxmysql

Installation
-------------
1. Download the latest release from your Cfx.re Keymaster.
2. Place the resource into your server's resources folder.
3. Configure your Finnhub API key in config.lua.
4. Add "ensure lumi_stockhub" to your server.cfg.
5. Restart your server.
